Visualization Information

What you are seeing is a partial visualization of a much larger dataset. The ticks along the outside of the circle represent a book.

Each line represents a connection between two books. A connection exists if a reviewer made a review for both books.

Along the bottom are three different charts. The stacked chart shows a comparison of the amount of 1-5 star reviews a book received.

The bar chart shows the average stars a book received, compared to other selected books.

The scatterplot shows how many reviews a book got with what its average rating is.

Hovering over any of the chart's books will highlight where that book is in the chart.

A book may appear in multiple places due to the fact that books can have multiple genres.

You may search for books on the right. Results will show up underneath. Clicking a book will add it to the selected books.

Clicking the same book in either the search results, or the selected books, will remove it from the selected books.
